Whiteboard

Whiteboard is a user collaboration platform that allows users to store projects, track todos, and collaborate on a whiteboard-like interface with "sticky notes".
For this project I was a a part of a software development team, and was the UX design lead. As such, I worked with my team to create an interface that could be designed within our time frame and skill set.
The goal for this mockup was to create a simplistic project management platform that uses visuals to organize tasks and ideas. As such, I wanted a clean, sharp interface that was also energetic. I chose a color scheme that could be used to catagorize the urgency of tasks and group ideas on the whiteboard. In addition, I paid attention to ensuring colors were accessible.
